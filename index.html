The Shard

The Shard - London's iconic skyscraper, designed by architect Renzo Piano, soars 310 meters (1,016 feet)
into the sky. It offers a 360-degree panoramic view from the 72nd-floor observation deck, showcasing
landmarks like Tower Bridge, St. Paul's Cathedral, and the River Thames.

What sets The Shard apart is not just its striking design and views, but also its multifunctionality.
It houses offices, luxury apartments, world-class restaurants, and a five-star hotel. Dining at Aqua Shard or
Hutong while taking in the breathtaking cityscape is a culinary experience unlike any other. Beyond its
architecture, The Shard seamlessly blends history with modernity, making it a must-visit in the heart of London.
